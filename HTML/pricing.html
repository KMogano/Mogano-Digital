Our Professional Delivery Process

Technical precision takes time. Here is how we ensure your digital infrastructure is built to last.

Discovery & Briefing

Days 1-3

The "Green Light" Phase: Once the deposit is paid and your content (logo, text, images) is received, we conduct a technical consultation to align on your requirements and project goals.

Key Milestone: Finalise Project Brief

Infrastructure & UI Design

Days 4-7

The "Foundation" Phase: We configure your hosting environment, secure your domain, and design the visual layout. This stage ensures the "look and feel" is approved before we begin the technical build.

Key Milestone: Design & Layout Approval

Development

Days 8-15

The "Build" Phase: We turn the approved design into a functional, high-performance system. This includes full mobile optimization and setting up our signature "Security-First" backend infrastructure.

Key Milestone: Functional Website Alpha

Technical QA & Deployment

Days 16-20

The "Launch" Phase: We conduct rigorous bug testing, SEO indexing, and security audits. The process concludes with a 30-minute training session to hand over your new digital asset.

Key Milestone: Officaial Site Launch & Handover

"Note: Timelines are calculated from the date all project assets (text, images, and branding) are received. As a boutique agency focused on quality, we limit the number of active projects we take on to ensure your system receives the technical attention it deserves."
